About ZeroAds

Our mission: A better web for everyone

Our Story

ZeroAds was born from a simple frustration: the modern web had become cluttered with intrusive advertisements, tracking scripts, and pop-ups that made browsing slow, distracting, and privacy-invasive.

We asked ourselves: "What if we could bring back the clean, fast web that we all remember?" That's when ZeroAds was created.

Our goal was simple: build the most effective ad blocker with the simplest interface. No complex settings, no premium tiers, no data collection. Just pure, ad-free browsing for everyone.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves to browse the web without:

  • Intrusive Ads - Pop-ups, banners, and video ads that interrupt your experience
  • Privacy Invasion - Tracking scripts that follow you across the web
  • Slow Loading - Heavy ad networks that make pages crawl
  • Distractions - Flashing animations and auto-play videos

ZeroAds is our commitment to making the web better, faster, and more private for everyone.

Why ZeroAds is Different

1. Privacy First

Unlike many other ad blockers, we don't collect any data. No browsing history, no analytics, no tracking. Your privacy is not for sale.

2. Truly Free

No premium versions, no subscriptions, no "acceptable ads" whitelist for payment. ZeroAds is completely free for everyone, forever.

3. Simple Design

One toggle button. That's it. No overwhelming settings pages or complicated configurations. It just works.

4. Powerful Blocking

We use Chrome's latest Manifest V3 technology with declarativeNetRequest API for fast, efficient, browser-level blocking.

5. Open & Transparent

We believe in transparency. Our code is available for review, and we're open about how we block ads and protect your privacy.

Technology

ZeroAds is built on cutting-edge web technologies:

  • Manifest V3: Chrome's latest extension platform for better security and performance
  • declarativeNetRequest API: Browser-level request blocking for maximum efficiency
  • Content Scripts: DOM-level element removal for thorough ad cleaning
  • Local Storage: All data stays on your device, never transmitted
