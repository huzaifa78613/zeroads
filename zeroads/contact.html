Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I install ZeroAds?

Visit the Chrome Web Store, search for "ZeroAds", and click "Add to Chrome". The extension will install automatically.

Is ZeroAds really free?

Yes! ZeroAds is completely free with no premium features, subscriptions, or hidden costs. All features are free forever.

Does ZeroAds collect my data?

No. We don't collect any personal data, browsing history, or any other information. Everything stays on your device.

Why isn't it blocking ads on a specific site?

Some websites use new ad technologies. Please report the site to us and we'll add it to our blocking rules.

Can I use ZeroAds on mobile?

Currently ZeroAds works on desktop Chrome. Mobile support is planned for future releases.

How do I disable ZeroAds temporarily?

Click the ZeroAds icon in your toolbar and toggle it OFF. You can re-enable it anytime with the same toggle.
